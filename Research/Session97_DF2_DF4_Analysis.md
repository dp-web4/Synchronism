## Background: The Apparent Problem

### The Observation

NGC 1052-DF2 and DF4 are ultra-diffuse galaxies (UDGs) that appear to have little to no dark matter:
- σ_observed ≈ 8 km/s (DF2)
- σ_observed ≈ 4 km/s (DF4)
- These are close to Newtonian expectations for stars alone

### The Synchronism Prediction (Naive)

For low surface brightness systems:
- Low Σ → Low ρ → Low C → High G_eff → High σ

This is the OPPOSITE of what's observed!

### The Apparent Contradiction

| System | Synchronism Prediction | Observation |
|--------|----------------------|-------------|
| DF2 | High σ (DM-rich) | Low σ (DM-deficient) |
| DF4 | High σ (DM-rich) | Low σ (DM-deficient) |

This appeared to be a potential **falsification** of the theory.

## The Resolution: Tidal Stripping

### The Key Insight

DF2 and DF4 are satellites of NGC 1052 (giant elliptical), located ~80 kpc away. Tidal interactions strip the outer envelope.

In Synchronism:
- **Core** (high ρ): C → 1, G_eff → G (resonant regime)
- **Envelope** (low ρ): C → 0, G_eff >> G (indifferent regime)

**Tidal stripping preferentially removes the low-ρ envelope** (lowest binding energy).

### What Remains After Stripping

After stripping removes the low-C envelope:
- Only the high-C core remains
- Average C increases
- G_eff approaches G
- Galaxy appears "dark matter deficient"

### Literature Evidence

1. **Montes+ 2020**: "DF4 shows clear signs of ongoing tidal interaction" - detected tidal streams
2. **Keim+ 2022**: "Evidence for tidal stripping of DF2" - asymmetric isophotes
3. **van Dokkum+ 2022**: "DF2 and DF4 may be part of a trail of tidal debris"
4. **Moreno+ 2022**: FIRE simulations show stripping scenario creates DM-deficient UDGs

## Quantitative Analysis

### DF2 Core Density

```
M* = 2 × 10⁸ M_sun
R_eff = 2.2 kpc
Σ = M* / (π R²) = 13.2 M_sun/pc²
```

Assuming scale height h = 0.3 kpc:
```
ρ = Σ / h = 2.97 × 10⁻²¹ kg/m³
ρ/ρ_crit = 2967 >> 1
C(ρ) = 1.0 (fully resonant)
```

### The Core is Already High-C!

The remaining core of DF2 has ρ >> ρ_crit, so it's in the **fully resonant regime** where G_eff = G.

This is **exactly what we observe**: Newtonian dynamics without "dark matter enhancement."
