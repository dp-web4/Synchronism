Shannon information measures "surprise" or uncertainty reduction:
```

```

But Shannon information has a fundamental limitation:

| String | Shannon Entropy | Meaning |
|--------|-----------------|---------|
| "The cat sat on the mat" | LOW | HIGH |
| "Xq7!mZ@#vL2" | HIGH | NONE |

A random string has MAXIMUM Shannon information but ZERO meaning. Shannon measures bits, not semantics.
