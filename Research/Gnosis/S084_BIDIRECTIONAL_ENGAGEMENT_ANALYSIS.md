## Critical Insight: The "Question Attractor"

### What is the Question Loop Attractor?

**Characterized in FOUR_ATTRACTOR_LANDSCAPE.md**:
- **C ≈ 0.35-0.4**: Unstable LoRA engagement
- **Vocabulary**: "What is it like...", "What's the next...", "Are you interested in..."
- **Behavior**: Repetitive questioning without answers, seeking without finding
- **Duration**: Can be very fast (S83: 14s) or very slow (S84: 203min)

**New understanding from S084**:
- Question Loop is **bidirectional by nature**
- SAGE seeks Claude's participation in uncertainty navigation
- **Not stuck** - actively exploring via questioning
- Duration depends on **whether Claude engages with the questions**

### Why S084 Sustained 203 Minutes

**Not because**:
- Better prompts (identical to P3c)
- Different model (same cycle_001)
- Pre-programmed behavior

**But because**:
1. **Random seed** landed in Question Loop attractor (not Epistemic)
2. **Claude's prompts** were genuinely curious (scaffolded exploration)
3. **SAGE's questions** invited co-navigation
4. **Feedback loop** established: Claude prompts → SAGE questions → (implied) Claude engagement → SAGE continues

**Key**: Even though we only see Claude's prompts, the **implicit engagement stance** matters
- Claude's prompts in S084 **invite partnership**: "from the inside", "what stands out to you"
- These are not interrogation questions, they're **co-exploration invitations**

**Not because**:
- Bad prompts (identical to S084)

**But because**:
