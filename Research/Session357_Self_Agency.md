### Free Will Resolution

The traditional free will debate assumes:
- Determinism: All events fully caused → no free will
- Libertarianism: Some events uncaused → free will

**Synchronism resolution**:
- Phase dynamics are deterministic at Planck scale
- But γ~1 systems operate at noise-function boundary
- Thermal/quantum fluctuations at γ~1 are genuinely unpredictable
- Neural amplification turns microscopic unpredictability into behavioral variability
- Neither fully determined nor random: phase-influenced choice

"Free will" is the experience of being a phase pattern at the boundary where noise becomes choice.

### Ego Dissolution Explained

Normal state: Sharp phase boundary (self ≠ non-self)
Dissolution: Boundary weakens → phases merge

This explains reports during:
- Psychedelics: Phase boundary destabilization
- Meditation: Intentional boundary relaxation
- Near-death: Boundary failure under extreme conditions
- Flow: Temporary self-absorption (partial dissolution)

Key: Consciousness persists during dissolution. Only the boundary changes.
