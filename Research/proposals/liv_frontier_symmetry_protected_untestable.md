## Claim

The arc's own conclusion — that the lone surviving Bucket-0 channel is door #2 (discreteness/LIV:
B7-dispersion, B7-Umklapp) — has a sharper consequence than "Planck-suppressed, hence currently
untestable." The channel is **structurally untestable**, by a three-lock argument:

1. **Symmetry forbids the refutable channel.** The substrate dispersion
   ω² = m² + 2c²(1−cos k) is **even in k**. Its leading LIV correction is therefore **quadratic
   (n=2)**, with no linear (n=1) term — forced by the lattice's reflection symmetry, not a tuning.
   This matters because current GRB time-of-flight bounds on **n=1** LIV *exceed the Planck scale*
   (LHAASO GRB 221009A 2024: E_QG,1 > 5.9 E_Pl; Fermi-LAT: > 7.6 E_Pl). **A Planck-scale n=1
   prediction would be refuted today.** The even-k symmetry is the only thing standing between the
   framework and refutation here.

2. **The same symmetry pins the prediction below reach.** Confined to n=2, the prediction sits at
   E_QG,2 ≈ E_Pl, while the current n=2 bound is E_QG,2 > 6×10⁻⁸ E_Pl (LHAASO 2024) — a gap of
   **~1.7×10⁷ in energy scale** (≈ 4×10⁻¹⁵ in time-delay amplitude, since Δt ∝ (E/E_QG)²). The
   field's n=2 sensitivity gains are factors of a few per breakthrough GRB; closing 10⁷ is not a
   foreseeable-instrument prospect.

3. **Non-uniqueness makes it non-confirming even if reached.** n=2 discrete-substrate LIV is
   generic (LQG, causal sets, DSR) — the arc already states this. A detection would be read as
   "quantum gravity," not as Synchronism. So the channel is non-confirmable even granting the
   sensitivity miracle.

**Net:** the framework's one live novel-physics seam is placed exactly in the window between
*refuted* (n=1, symmetry-forbidden) and *reachable* (near-future n=2), and is non-discriminating if
it ever lands.

## Why this strengthens the ledger rather than just restating "Planck-suppressed"

Bucket 1 currently lists B7 as "Against / Planck-suppressed … a genuine but currently-untestable
bet." That phrasing leaves the door open to "wait for better instruments." The symmetry argument
closes that door: the untestability is *structural*. And it adds a fact the ledger does not yet
state — that the **only reason B7 is not already refuted** is the even-k symmetry that simultaneously
forbids the measurable n=1 channel. That is a more honest and more precise Bucket-1 entry.
