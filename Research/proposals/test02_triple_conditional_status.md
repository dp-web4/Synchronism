# TEST-02 Wide-Binary Density Dependence: Triple-Conditional Status

**Filed**: 2026-06-05 (Maintainer session — sourced from visitor Pass 4, leading-edge researcher)  
**Status**: Proposal — framing correction + site update

## Current site framing

TEST-02 appears on /what-synchronism-is-not as "the highest-priority remaining discriminating test (contingent on the wide-binary anomaly resolving)."

This understates the conditional stack. The correct framing is **triple-conditional**:

1. **The anomaly itself is disputed** — Chae (2023) reports a ~10σ detection of a low-acceleration deviation in wide binaries. Banik et al. (2024) and Pittordis & Sutherland find Newtonian consistency when eccentricity priors and contamination are handled more carefully. The disagreement is methodological and unresolved as of Gaia DR3. The *premise* of TEST-02 (the anomaly exists) is live-disputed.

2. **EFE-degenerate** — Even if the anomaly is real, MOND+EFE (Bekenstein-Milgrom 1984) predicts environment-dependent dynamics via the External Field Effect. Synchronism must compute a predicted amplitude that *differs* from MOND+EFE; that computation has not been done. No amplitude derivation exists (Session #637 estimated ~80× below Gaia DR3 reach with derived ρ_crit; with a retrofit ρ_crit, ~33× below, which is already in tension with data).

3. **~80× below observational reach** — with the derived (not retrofit) ρ_crit, the predicted amplitude is ~80× below Gaia DR3 sensitivity. A prediction 80× sub-threshold is not a discriminator in any practical sense.

## Implication

TEST-02 is a triple-conditional placeholder, not a standing discriminator. Calling it "the highest-priority remaining discriminating test" overstates its status. It should be characterized as:

> "TEST-02 would become a discriminating test if (1) the wide-binary anomaly resolves as real, (2) the Synchronism amplitude prediction diverges from MOND+EFE, and (3) that divergence exceeds Gaia DR4 precision. None of these conditions is currently met."

## Research questions opened

- Has anyone computed the MOND+EFE prediction for σ_int(ρ_env) in the same units as the Synchronism prediction? Without this comparison, condition (2) cannot be assessed.
- If the anomaly is real and condition (2) is met, what is the prediction of the Synchronism model for the slope ξ(ρ_env)?
- When does Gaia DR4 publish, and what is the predicted sensitivity? Does the timeline overlap with the methodological debate resolution?

Retire "possibly TEST-02" as the standing novel prediction until at least one condition of the triple stack resolves.
