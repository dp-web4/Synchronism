## The Sharper Statement

**C is a latent variable, not an observable.** Every construction is a *forward map*: a measured
input → a *predicted* C that is never measured as an output and checked. The single exception is
the galaxy rung, where C = g_bar/g_obs is the RAR observable — but that is the prediction target
itself (not an independent test), and the C(ρ) prediction of it is refuted. The one *independent*
C-measurement attempt (Reynolds-number form) is internally inconsistent by 440× before any data
contact. Consciousness-C has measurable inputs (D, S) but an unwritten function f.

This is stronger than "unmeasurable," which invites "wait for an instrument." The real asymmetry:
**where C is measurable it is refuted; where it is not refuted it is not measurable.** Same
refutable-not-confirmable structure as the wide-binary sector and the same
re-execute-don't-re-trust lesson as DESI/A-from-Jeans, now at the level of the central quantity.

## Two Constructive Threads (not closures)

1. **The CFD Reynolds form is the most interesting dead end** — the only reach for an independent
   C-measurement, failed at self-consistency not at data. One focused session could settle whether
   the 440× is a fixable normalization or a structural impossibility (three thresholds encoding
   incompatible flow regimes). If structural, it is the *measurement* twin of the locality no-go.
2. **C_conv is the framework's one working C-from-data procedure** — and it works precisely because
   the trust domain supplies a measurable (inter-agent agreement) that is *not* the prediction
   target. The physics C's problem may be structural to forward-map dark-matter mimics: their only
   natural C-measurable (mass discrepancy) *is* the target.
