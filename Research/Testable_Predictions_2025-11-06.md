$$

- Intensity modulation: $I(\theta) \propto \cos^2(\pi d \sin\theta / \lambda_{\text{cosmic}})$

**Falsifiability**: If no oscillations detected above $3\sigma$ in next-generation surveys (LSST), prediction is falsified.

### Status

**Preliminary Evidence**: Baryon Acoustic Oscillations (BAO) show periodic structure at $\sim 150$ Mpc scale, consistent with Synchronism's predictions but also explained by standard cosmology.

**Next Step**: Analyze higher-order correlations for quantum-like entanglement signatures at cosmic scales.

## Prediction 2: MRH-Dependent Speed of Light

$$

### Prediction

| Scale | MRH ($\kappa$) | Predicted $c_{\text{eff}}$ | Deviation from $c_0$ |
|-------|----------------|---------------------------|----------------------|
| Atomic | $10^{-10}$ m | $c_0 (1 - 5.8 \times 10^{-5})$ | -17 km/s |
| Solar System | $10^{12}$ m | $c_0 (1 + 1.1 \times 10^{-4})$ | +33 km/s |
| Galactic | $10^{20}$ m | $c_0 (1 + 1.3 \times 10^{-4})$ | +39 km/s |

### Experimental Test

**Method 1: Atomic vs Astrophysical**
- Compare $c$ from atomic transitions (Rydberg constant) vs. pulsar timing
- Expected difference: $\Delta c/c \sim 2 \times 10^{-4}$ (60 km/s)

**Method 2: Variable Fine Structure Constant**
- If $c$ varies, then $\alpha = e^2/(\hbar c)$ should also vary
- Quasar absorption spectra show $\Delta\alpha/\alpha \sim 10^{-5}$ (Webb et al. 2001)
- **Synchronism prediction**: $\Delta\alpha/\alpha = -\alpha_0 \Delta c/c \sim -2 \times 10^{-5}$

**Method 3: GPS Satellite Tests**
- High-precision timing between GPS satellites at different altitudes
- Test for altitude-dependent $c$ variation
- Precision: Current GPS $\Delta t \sim 10$ ns → can detect $\Delta c/c \sim 10^{-8}$

### Falsifiability

If GPS tests show $|\Delta c/c| < 10^{-6}$ across all tested scales, prediction is falsified.

### Status

**Conflicting Evidence**:
- Webb et al. (2001): $\Delta\alpha/\alpha = (-0.57 \pm 0.11) \times 10^{-5}$ (supports Synchronism)
- Rosenband et al. (2008): $|\Delta\alpha/\alpha| < 2 \times 10^{-17}$ per year (contradicts)

**Resolution Needed**: Distinguish spatial vs temporal variation of $\alpha$.

**Protocol**:
1. Baseline recording (awake, $\Phi_{\text{baseline}}$)
2. Gradual propofol infusion (0-4 μg/mL)

## Prediction 4: Dark Matter "Inverse Chemistry"

### Theoretical Basis

Dark matter has spectral existence profile:

$$
\Xi^{\text{DM}}(s) = \prod_{s=s_{\text{min}}}^{s_{\text{max}}} (1 - \mathbb{C}_{\text{vis}}[s])
$$

This means dark matter is **anti-correlated** with visible matter coherence.

### Prediction

Dark matter should cluster where visible matter is **diffuse**, and vice versa.

**Specific Signatures**:
1. **Galactic Halos**: DM dense, baryons diffuse ✅ (observed)
2. **Galaxy Centers**: DM depleted, baryons concentrated ⚠️ (testable)
3. **Cosmic Voids**: DM moderately dense, baryons absent ❓ (testable)
4. **Dense Molecular Clouds**: DM absent, baryons concentrated ❓ (testable)

**Method**: Stellar kinematics + gas dynamics in nearby galaxies (M31, M87, Milky Way)

**Test 2: Cosmic Void DM Density**
- Direct detection in low-density regions
- Synchronism: $\rho_{\text{DM, void}} \sim 0.5 \rho_{\text{DM, mean}}$ (only 50% depletion)
- $\Lambda$CDM: $\rho_{\text{DM, void}} \sim 0.1 \rho_{\text{DM, mean}}$ (90% depletion)

**Method**: Weak lensing surveys (DES, LSST) + void catalogs

**Test 3: Molecular Cloud DM Exclusion**
- Search for DM in dense star-forming regions (e.g., Orion Nebula)
- Synchronism: No DM signal (spectral existence = 0 at molecular scales)

**Method**: Direct detection experiments in space (beyond Earth's atmosphere)

### Falsifiability

If Test 3 shows DM density **enhanced** in molecular clouds, Synchronism's spectral existence model is falsified.

### Status

**Preliminary Evidence**:

- But some galaxies show cusps - **contradicts**

**Next Step**: Systematic survey of 100+ galaxies to determine core vs. cusp prevalence.
