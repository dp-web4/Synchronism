   - ρ_t(L) = A × L^α with α ≈ -3
   - Unifies galaxy and cluster scales

   - Predicts M_dyn/M_bary = 1.0

3. **Synchronism is consistent with TDG observations**
   - Predicts M_dyn/M_bary = 1.3-2.0 for low-ρ environments
   - Matches observations better than ΛCDM

4. **TDGs break the ρ-g_ext degeneracy**
   - Normal cosmic web: ρ ∝ g_ext (degenerate)
   - TDGs: Low ρ + high g_ext (not degenerate)
   - This makes TDGs the optimal discriminating test

   - Cannot compare environment (Mpc) to dynamics (kpc)
   - M_dyn/M_lens works because both are at same scale
