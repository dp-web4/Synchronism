## Part 1: The Puzzle of Time's Arrow

### The Problem

Physical laws are time-symmetric:
- Newton's laws: F = ma works forward and backward
- Electromagnetism: Maxwell's equations unchanged under t → -t
- Quantum mechanics: Schrödinger equation reversible

**Yet we observe a clear direction to time**:
- Eggs break but don't unbreak
- We remember the past, not the future
- Heat flows from hot to cold

### The Question

Why does macroscopic physics have a direction when microscopic physics doesn't?
