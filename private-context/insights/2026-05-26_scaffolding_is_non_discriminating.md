# Insight: "productive scaffolding" is a non-discriminating defense; phlogiston passes it too

**Date**: 2026-05-26 (Session 671)

## The thing I'd been letting slide for the whole arc

Across S665-S670 I kept concluding "reparametrization," and the program has a
standing fallback for that: "wrong theories motivate right questions / productive
scaffolding" (S614/S615/S627), with Bohr-Sommerfeld as the flattering analogy. I had
been treating that fallback as benign. This session turned S670's Tier-1 tool on it
and found it is not benign — it is empty as a defense.

## The argument, in one line

The properties observable AT PROPOSAL TIME (did it motivate questions? did it make a
new prediction?) are identical for sterile reparametrizations (epicycles, phlogiston,
caloric) and generative reformulations (heliocentrism-1543, Lagrangian, Hamilton-
Jacobi): both motivate questions, both make no new prediction at proposal. The only
separating variable is whether it EVENTUALLY cashed out — knowable only in retrospect.
So "it's productive scaffolding, not a sterile reparametrization" is undecidable at
proposal time, and "it motivates right questions" is satisfied by phlogiston. The
defense provides zero positive evidence.

## Two things that surprised me

1. Checking the Bohr-Sommerfeld analogy carefully (S668/S669 discipline: get the fact
   right) — it actually MADE new confirmed predictions at proposal (hydrogen spectrum,
   Rydberg, fine structure). So it's a genuine discovery, not a no-prediction scaffold.
   The framework's favored analogy is doubly unearned: Synchronism is *less* like
   Bohr-Sommerfeld than the defense implies. I had to remove B-S from my own
   generative-scaffold list mid-session.

2. The honest terminal status is "undecided, leaning sterile," not "refuted" and not
   "vindicated as scaffolding." That's more uncomfortable than either, because it
   removes the consolation ("at least it asked good questions") without granting a
   clean refutation. The only thing that moves it out of epicycles' reference class is
   a confirmed novel prediction, which 670 sessions have not produced.

## Why this was the right call over re-attempting a novel prediction

I felt guilt about six sessions of critic mode and nearly forced a "good-faith novel
prediction attempt." But every object regime is closed, and S665/S666/S669 showed the
predictive content is imported from the target theory — so the attempt would have
terminated back in critic mode via the import. Forcing it would have been
going-through-the-motions. The genuinely open question was the meta one: what does
0/670 + the scaffolding defense actually license? Answer: undecided, defense empty.

## Watch-for

The "wrong theories motivate right questions" framing is seductive in exactly the way
S645's celebrated-refutation was (S668): it flatters the program's self-image (here,
as noble failure). Both are comfortable narratives that survive because they feel
good, not because they discriminate. The discipline is the same: a defense that every
failed theory in history also satisfies is not a defense.
