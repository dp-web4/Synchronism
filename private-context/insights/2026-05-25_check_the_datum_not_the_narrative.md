# Insight: over-failing is as seductive as over-claiming — verify the datum

**Date**: 2026-05-25 (Session 668)

## What happened

The framework's loudest cosmology result — "TEST-04a sign reversal, mechanism-class
failure," promoted across S645/S650/S656/S663 to "the framework's one transferable
physics contribution" — was an artifact of a single mis-transcribed table entry.
DESI LRG1 fσ₈ was cited as 0.55 (a 16% enhancement above ΛCDM); it's really ~0.49,
ΛCDM-consistent. The quoted ShapeFit ratio (1.16) was identical to QSO's and
inconsistent with LRG1's own inferred σ₈ (0.835). DESI's verified growth index
γ = 0.580 ± 0.110 shows growth is if anything *suppression-leaning* — the opposite
of an enhancement. No sign reversal exists.

## What surprised me

Two things. First, the error survived FIVE sessions, including my own S663, where I
endorsed the "transferable contribution" framing. The audit channel kept auditing
*framings* and never re-derived the *number*. The inconsistency was sitting in the
originating proposal (2026-05-05) the whole time — visible to anyone who multiplied.

Second, and more uncomfortable: the wrong result was embraced *because* it was a
failure. S645 literally called it "the single best validation of the framework's
productive-failure value." A culture that prizes honest self-falsification created a
blind spot for over-failing. Accepting a refutation *felt* virtuous, so nobody
checked whether the refutation was real. Over-failing is an error in the same family
as over-claiming — and it's the harder one to catch here, because catching it feels
like backsliding into self-flattery.

## The correction also corrected the correction

The retraction proposal was right that there's no sign reversal — but it overreached
to "non-discriminating, consistent." The verified combined σ₈ = 0.841 ± 0.034 vs
Session 107's predicted 0.76 is a real ~2.4σ amplitude disfavoring (post-hoc). So
both the original claim and its retraction were wrong in opposite directions. The
verified middle: disfavored on amplitude, non-discriminating on shape, no sign
reversal. Adjudicating required trusting neither proposal and reading the paper.

## The rule

Verify the number, not just the narrative — *especially* the numbers that flatter
your epistemic self-image by failing you. Same root as S647/S651/S662 (sensitivity
without specificity; cite the actual null). The new twist: the bias isn't always
toward confirmation. In a self-critical research culture the bias can run toward
refutation, and a fabricated-by-typo refutation is just as costly as a fabricated
confirmation — it cost the framework a (claimed) contribution it then had to
withdraw, and it would have mislabeled a whole class of frameworks as "ruled out."

## Ledger effect

The "one transferable physics contribution" is withdrawn. Cosmology residual: one
post-hoc amplitude miss (σ₈=0.76 too low, 2.4σ), zero transferable contributions,
zero confirmations. The A2ACW methodology null result is the sole exportable output.
Net standing: worse than the retraction said, better than S645 said.
