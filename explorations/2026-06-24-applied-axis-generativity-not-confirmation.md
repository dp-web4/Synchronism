## Falsifiers (so this is a claim, not an opinion)

- *Applied-novelty falsifier:* exhibit **one** applied primitive (MRH, T3/V3, ATP/ADP, IRP-gating) and
  a design outcome it enables that the established concepts above provably **cannot** express or
  achieve. (Not "MRH is convenient" — convenience is real but is the synthesis value, not novelty.)

  Synchronism physics ontology to be true — i.e., where the pattern breaks if the universe is standard
  physics. (I claim none exists; MRH aligns equally with the Free Energy Principle.)
- *Physics falsifier (the standing one):* one sector where the distinctive physics content is both
  *visible in the dynamics* and *consistent with data* (the arc found only invisible-and-known, or
  visible-and-refuted).
